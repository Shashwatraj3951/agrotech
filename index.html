How AgroTech Works?

Smart Farming

AgroTech uses farm level data to predict ideal growth conditions and resource requirements - including irrigation, sprays, fertigation and other preventive measures and notify farmers to take informed decisions. AgroTech monitors micro & macro climatic conditions, below the soil parameters, solar conditions, crop stage, crop growth characteristics, etc.

Irrigation Alert

AgroTech continuously checks water availability in the soil to ensure that the irrigation requirement of the crop is precisely met at all timesbased on crop, its stage, and soil characteristics.

Fertilizer Alert

AgroTech continuously checks sodium, pottasium, phosphorus, soil moisture and temperature availability in the soil to ensure that the fertilizer requirement of the crop is precisely met at all timesbased on crop, its stage, and soil characteristics.

Weather Forecast

AgroTech provides a farm-specific micro-climatic forecast for next 14 days to keep you well prepared for weather risks of the future.

Disease & Pest Alerts

AgroTech disease prediction and assessment systems forewarn farmers and agri institutions about the possibility of a crop disease, it's severity, possibility of pest outbreak so that the farmers can precisely time their preventive sprays.

Record Activities & Finances

AgroTech provides real-time insight into daily progress of your crops and activities, manages sales, expenses and cash flow to ensure the health of your finances.
